A Cooperative Action Game for Android and iOS
Coming August 2020

Cosmic radiation is tearing through your ship, you and your friends are the only donuts who can stop it! Donut waste time as you roll and patch your way through these bite-sized holes in your ship. You’ll need to communicate and cooperate to achieve your dream of a sweet, sweet space.

Tilt

Tilt your phone left and right to roll your small donut around the ship. Roll over breaches of your color to fix them.

Tap

Tap the screen to jump over obstacles.

Talk

Talk to your shipmates to ask for help.

Hull Breaches

Certain breaches can only be patched by certain members

Locked Doors

Doors can only be opened with the help of two members

Work together. Save your ship.
