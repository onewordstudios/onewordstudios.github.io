Tilt

Tilt your phone left and right to roll your donut around the ship. Roll back-and-forth over breaches of your color to fix them.

Tap

Tap the screen to jump over obstacles.

Talk

Talk to your shipmates to ask for help.

Hull Breaches

Certain breaches can only be patched by certain members

Locked Doors

Doors can only be opened with the help of two members

Engine Malfunctions

Jump on both buttons together to restart the engine

Stabilizer Failures

Get everyone to roll in the same direction to stabilize the ship
